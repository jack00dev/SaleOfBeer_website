About Us

At Beer House in Adakpamé, Lomé, we pride ourselves on being a go-to destination for beer lovers and conservation seekers alike. We're dedicated to providing an exceptional beer experience in a casy and inviting atmosphere, where guests can relax, unwind, and enjoy their time in comfort.

OUR COLLECTION

  • Pils Beer

    Pils, the beer that brings people together. With its authentic taste and unmatched freshness, Pils is the perfect companion for good times with friends or family.

  • Castel Beer

    Castel Beer, the perfect blend of freshness and togetherness. Recognized across Africa, Castel Beer elevates every moment of sharing. Its delicate flavor and refreshing character make it the ideal choice for gatherings with friends or family.

  • Bollinger Champagne

    Bollinger Special Cuvée, a champagne with character. Refined, intense, and elegant in every glass. Perfect for life's finest moments.

  • Cognac XO Imperial

    Cognac XO Imperial, richness and elegance in every glass. Deep aromas, silky texture, refined finish. Aged slowly to reveal its full complexity. Each sip is an invitation to a sensory journey.

  • Imperial Blue Whisky

    Imperial Blue, the elegance of refined whisky. Discover a balanced whisky blending smoothness and complexity. Width its woody and subtly spicy notes, Imperial Blue captivates those seeking an authentic, refined experience. Perfect for relaxing moments or evenings with friends.

  • Ron Diplomatico

    Ron Diplomatico, the art of exceptional rum. This Venezuelan rum is renowned for its rich, smooth profile with notes of caramel, vanilla, and dried fruits. Slowly aged in oak barrels, it delivers a unique aromatic complexity and a long, velvety finish. Perfect for sipping neat or crafting sophisticated cocktails.
